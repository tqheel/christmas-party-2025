# Cocktail Menu

## Old Fashioned

**Ingredients**

- 2 oz B6 proof Old Forester bourbon
- 1 tsp Simple Syrup
- 1 tsp Bing Cherry juice
- 2 dashes Remedy Noir Bitters
- 1 Bing cherry

**Steps**

1.  Combine the simple syrup, cherry juice, and bitters in a rocks
    glass.
2.  Add the whiskey and stir briefly.
3.  Fill the glass with large ice cubes.
4.  Stir for 20--30 seconds to achieve proper dilution and chilling.
5.  Garnish with a Bing cherry.

## Manhattan

**Ingredients**

- 2 oz 86 proof Old Forester bourbon
- 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
- 2 dashes Remedy Noir Bitters
- 1 Bing cherry

**Steps**

1.  Add whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters into a mixing glass with
    ice.
2.  Stir until well-chilled (approximately 30 seconds).
3.  Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.
4.  Garnish with a Bing cherry.

## Espresso Martini

**Ingredients**

- 2 oz Vodka
- 1 oz Freshly brewed espresso (or very strong cold brew coffee)
- 0.5 oz Kahlúa
- 2 dashes Crude Big Bear Coffee and Cocoa Bitters
- Coffee beans for garnish (optional)

**Steps**

1.  Combine vodka, coffee, Kahlúa, and cocoa bitters in a cocktail
    shaker.
2.  Fill the shaker with plenty of ice.
3.  Shake vigorously for 15 seconds to create a thick foam.
4.  Double strain into a chilled martini glass.
5.  Garnish with three coffee beans if desired.

## Chocolate Martini

**Ingredients**

- 1.5 oz Vanilla Vodka
- 1.5 oz Baileys Chocolate Liqueur
- 0.5 oz Crème de Cacao

**Steps**

1.  Add vodka, Baileys, and crème de cacao to a cocktail shaker filled
    with ice.
2.  Shake well until chilled.
3.  Strain into a chilled martini glass.

## Gin Martini

**Ingredients**

- 2.5 oz Gin
- 0.5 oz Dry Vermouth
- Lemon twist or green olive for garnish

**Steps**

1.  Combine gin and dry vermouth in a mixing glass with ice.
2.  Stir for 30 seconds (stirring preserves the clarity and texture of
    the gin).
3.  Strain into a chilled martini glass.
4.  Garnish with a lemon twist or an olive.
