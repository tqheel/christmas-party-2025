# Cocktail Menu

## Classic Cocktails

### Old Fashioned

- B6 proof Old Forester bourbon
- Simple Syrup
- Bing Cherry juice
- Remedy Noir Bitters
- Bing cherry

### Manhattan

- 86 proof Old Forester bourbon
- Sweet Vermouth
- Remedy Noir Bitters
- Bing cherry

### Espresso Martini

- Vodka
- Freshly brewed espresso (or very strong cold brew coffee)
- Kahlúa
- Crude Big Bear Coffee and Cocoa Bitters
- Coffee beans (optional)

### Chocolate Martini

- Vanilla Vodka
- Baileys Chocolate Liqueur
- Crème de Cacao

### Gin Martini

- Gin
- Dry Vermouth
- Lemon twist or green olive

### Vodka Martini

- Vodka
- Dry Vermouth
- Lemon twist or green olive
