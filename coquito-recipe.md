### Ingredients:

- 2 (15 oz) cans cream of coconut
- 2 (14 oz) cans sweetened condensed milk
- 2 (12 oz) cans evaporated milk
- 2 cups white rum (optional, can be adjusted to taste)
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- Â½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- Cinnamon sticks for garnish

### Instructions:

1. In a blender, combine cream of coconut, sweetened condensed milk, and evaporated milk
2. Add vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and nutmeg
3. Blend until smooth and well combined (about 2-3 minutes)
4. Add rum if using, and blend briefly to combine
5. Pour mixture into glass bottles
6. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight
7. Shake well before serving
8. Serve chilled in small glasses, garnished with cinnamon sticks

Note: Store in refrigerator for up to 1 week. Shake well before each serving as mixture may separate.

Serves: 8-10 people
